Openings

We have several openings available at the N.1 Institute, where

you will work in collaboration with NTU (Nanyang Technological University) on neuromorphic vision, object recognition and tracking for low power applications, including work on cutting edge neuromorphic processors, spiking neural networks and FPGA deployment. You will predominantly work on event-based camera sensors, which are low power image sensors with high temporal resolution, high dynamic range aynchronous signal output.
